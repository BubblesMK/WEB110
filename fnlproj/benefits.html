How Does User Experience (UX) Impact Business

As we have previously read, as a developer/designer it is best to apply the principles of UX design to your future websites and computer applications. When you focus on the concerns and needs of the user this provides an environment for a positive user experience, and this leads to positive outcomes for the business. How? According to Design Studios, the impacts of UX design encourages the below:

  1. Increased Conversion Rates
  2. Higher User Retention
  3. Customer Loyalty
  4. Increased Return of Investment (ROI)
  5. Higher Rate of User Growth
  6. Increased In-House Productivity
  7. Search Engine Optimization Boost
  8. Avoid Future Design Hassles
  9. Delivering Good User Experience on All Devices and Platform

In summary, developers influence how users interact with websites and applications. By creating environments that promote positive user experiences and designing user-friendly products, businesses are impacted greatly. In the end, when you create a product, have fun with it but keep in mind the user and their needs.
